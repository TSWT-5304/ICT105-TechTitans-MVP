# Lab 06 Case Study Brief
## Case: PeerTutor – Student-to-Student Tutoring Marketplace
Rangsit University students often experience difficulties finding trustworthy peer tutors for challenging subjects, examination preparation, assignments, and coursework. Most students currently rely on Facebook groups, LINE groups, recommendations from friends, or senior students to locate tutors. These approaches are scattered across multiple platforms, making it difficult to compare tutors based on their qualifications, teaching experience, subject expertise, availability, hourly rates, and student reviews.

Student tutors also face challenges in promoting their tutoring services because there is no centralized platform where they can register, showcase their academic strengths, create professional tutor profiles, and receive tutoring requests from students.

To address these challenges, **PeerTutor** provides a centralized web-based tutoring marketplace that simplifies tutor discovery, improves trust through verified tutor profiles and reviews, streamlines tutoring request management, and supports transparent payment and administrative processes.

## Target Users
## Primary Users
### Students
- Search and filter tutors by subject, major, price, and rating.
- View tutor profiles before making decisions.
- Submit tutoring requests.
- Track tutoring request status.
- Complete online payments.
- View payment history.
- Leave ratings and reviews after tutoring sessions.

### Student Tutors
- Register as tutors.
- Submit tutor applications.
- Create and manage tutor profiles after administrator approval.
- Receive and manage tutoring requests.
- Monitor tutoring schedules.
- View payment status, earnings, and student reviews.

## Secondary Users
### Platform Administrators

Administrators are responsible for:
- Reviewing and approving tutor applications.
- Managing student and tutor accounts.
- Verifying student payments.
- Managing tutor earnings and platform commissions.
- Monitoring tutoring activities.
- Viewing dashboard summaries and platform analytics.
- Maintaining the quality, security, and reliability of the PeerTutor platform.

> **Note:** The initial MVP focuses on students enrolled in the Rangsit University International College (RIC). Support for additional faculties and academic programs may be introduced in future versions of the platform.

## Product Direction
PeerTutor is a responsive web-based student-to-student tutoring marketplace that provides a centralized platform for academic support within the university.

The platform allows students to search for tutors, compare tutor profiles, submit tutoring requests, monitor request progress, complete online payments, review payment history, and leave ratings after completed tutoring sessions.

Student tutors can register, submit tutor applications, create and manage tutor profiles after approval, receive tutoring requests, approve or reject requests, monitor payment status and earnings, and build their reputation through student reviews.

Administrators manage tutor applications, user accounts, student payment verification, tutor earnings, platform records, and overall platform performance through an administrative dashboard with analytics and reporting.
