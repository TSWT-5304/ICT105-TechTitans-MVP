# Product Concept

## 1. Project Title
PeerTutor – Student-to-Student Tutoring Marketplace

## 2. Target User

### Primary Users
- Rangsit University students who need academic tutoring support and want to find suitable peer tutors easily.
- Student tutors who want to promote their tutoring services, manage tutoring requests, and connect with students.

### Secondary User
- Platform administrators who approve tutors, manage user accounts, verify payments, manage platform records, and monitor overall platform activities.

---

## 3. Problem Statement

Students need an easier way to find qualified peer tutors because current tutor information is scattered across Facebook groups, Line groups, friends, and senior students. This makes it difficult for students to compare tutors' subjects, qualifications, availability, pricing, and reliability before making a decision.

At the same time, student tutors have limited opportunities to promote their tutoring services and reach students who need academic support. They often depend on personal networks or social media posts, making it difficult to manage tutoring requests, track sessions, and organize payments.

---

## 4. Proposed IT Product

PeerTutor is a web-based student-to-student tutoring marketplace that allows students to search and filter tutors, view tutor profiles, submit tutoring requests, track request status, complete payment, and provide reviews after tutoring sessions.

The platform also allows student tutors to register, submit tutor applications, create profiles after approval, manage tutoring requests, and track payment status. Administrators can approve tutors, manage user accounts, verify payments, record platform commission, and monitor platform activities.

---

## 5. Core Value Proposition

PeerTutor centralizes tutor discovery, tutoring requests, and payment tracking in one trusted platform. It replaces scattered and unreliable tutor searches through social media and personal networks with searchable tutor profiles, structured requests, transparent status tracking, and review-based decision making.

The platform also provides student tutors with a dedicated space to promote their services, manage tutoring sessions, and receive organized payment records.

---

## 6. MVP Scope

The MVP focuses on essential features required to validate a university-based tutoring marketplace:

- Homepage / Landing Page
- Tutor Search and Filter
- Tutor Profile Viewing
- Tutoring Request Submission
- Request Confirmation and Status Tracking
- Tutor Registration and Application
- Tutor Approval Workflow
- Tutor Profile Management
- Student Dashboard
- Tutor Dashboard
- Admin Dashboard
- Online Payment Simulation
- Payment Status Tracking and Payment History
- Student Review and Rating
- Basic Platform Analytics

---

## 7. Out of Scope for Current Semester

The following features are excluded from the current semester prototype because they require additional technologies, infrastructure, or development time:

- Real-time in-app chat between students and tutors
- AI-based tutor matching and personalized study plans
- Built-in video tutoring sessions
- Tutor introduction videos
- Native mobile application deployment
