# Product Concept

## 1. Project Title
PeerTutor – Student-to-Student Tutoring Marketplace 

## 2. Target User
### Primary user
- Rangsit University students who need academic tutoring support 
- Student tutors who want to offer tutoring services and promote themselves. 
### Secondary user
- Platform admin who approves tutors, manages accounts, and monitors platform activity.

## 3. Problem Statement
Students need an easier way to find qualified peer tutors because current tutor information is scattered across Facebook groups, Line groups, friends, and senior students, making it difficult to compare tutors' subjects, experience, availability, and trustworthiness before contacting them. 
At the same time, student tutors have faced limited opportunities to promote their tutoring services and connect with students who need academic support. They often rely on word-of-mouth, social media, or personal networks, making it difficult to reach more students and manage tutoring requests in an organized way. 

## 4. Proposed IT Product
A web-based tutoring marketplace that lets students search and filter tutors, view detailed tutor profiles, submit structured tutoring requests, and track request status while letting tutors register, get approved, manage their profile, and handle incoming requests, view student feedbacks and letting admins approve/reject tutors, manage user accounts, and view platform analytics. 

## 5. Core Value Proposition
PeerTutor centralizes tutor discovery, booking on one trusted platform, replacing scattered, unverifiable chat-group searches with searchable, comparable, and trackable tutor information for students while giving student tutors a dedicated platform to promote their tutoring services. 

## 6. MVP Scope
The MVP focuses on: homepage, tutor search & filter, tutor profile viewing, tutoring request submission, confirmation & status tracking, tutor registration & application, tutor application status, tutor profile management, admin approval & account management, admin dashboard analytics, and student review & rating. 

## 7. Out of Scope for Current Semester
- Real-time in-app chat between students and tutors
- AI-based tutor matching / personalized study plans
- Built-in video tutoring sessions
- Tutor introduction videos
- Native mobile app deployment
