# User Consent Statement
This prototype is created as part of the **ICT105 Fundamental Technology Entrepreneurship** course for educational purposes. 

The project, **PeerTutor – Student-to-Student Tutoring Marketplace**, is being developed by the TechTitans team to evaluate the usability and feasibility of the proposed solution.

You are invited to participate in a usability test of the prototype. During the session, you will be asked to complete several tasks and provide feedback about your experience. Your participation is completely voluntary, and you may stop the test at any time without any negative consequences.

The information collected will be used **only** to evaluate and improve the PeerTutor prototype and to support the project's academic reports and presentations. The collected data will **not** be used for commercial purposes or shared outside the course.

Please **do not provide sensitive personal information**, such as your national ID number, passport number, bank account details, credit card information, passwords, health information, or home address. If possible, you may use a nickname or anonymous identifier instead of your full name.

By participating in this usability test, you confirm that you understand the purpose of the study and agree to the collection and use of your feedback as described below.
## Data Collected
The prototype may collect the following information during usability testing:
- **Name, nickname, or anonymous participant ID**
- **User role** (Student, Student Tutor, or Administrator)
- **Task completion result** (Completed / Not Completed)
- **Feedback score** (1–5)
- **Interest level** (1–5)
- **Comments about usability and suggestions for improvement**
- **General observations recorded by the facilitator*
No sensitive personal or financial information is collected during the usability test.

## Data Use
The collected information will be used only for:
- Evaluating the usability of the PeerTutor prototype.
- Analysing customer validation results and project metrics.
- Improving the prototype before the next development stage.
- Supporting ICT105 laboratory reports, presentations, and final project documentation.
- Demonstrating customer validation and MVP decision-making as part of the academic assessment.
Only the project team and course instructors will access the collected testing data. The results will be presented in summary or aggregated form whenever possible, and individual participants will not be identified without their permission.

Participants may request that their feedback be removed from the project records before the final submission if it has not already been anonymised.
