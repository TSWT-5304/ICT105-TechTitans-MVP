# Lab 04 - User Persona

## 1. Persona Name
Mina, Second-Year ICT Student

## 2. User Type
University student seeking academic tutoring support

## 3. Background and Context
- Mina is a second-year ICT student at Rangsit University.
- She often needs extra academic support when preparing for exams, completing assignments, or working on programming-related projects.
- She usually searches for tutors through Facebook groups, Line groups, friends, and senior students, but the information is scattered and difficult to verify.
- She finds it hard to compare tutors' experience, availability, and ratings before deciding whom to contact.

## 4. Goals
- Find a qualified peer tutor for a specific subject without searching across multiple platforms.
- View tutor profiles, including subjects taught, experience, ratings, and availability before making a decision.
- Submit a tutoring request through one simple platform instead of contacting tutors individually through Line or Messenger.
- Receive academic support quickly to improve her understanding, complete assignments on time, and achieve better grades.
