# Customer Discovery Questions

# For Students (Looking for Academic Help)

## Project Title
PeerTutor – Student-to-Student Tutoring Marketplace

## Target Respondent
University students who have needed academic help, searched for tutors, or received tutoring services.

## Consent and Context
1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT105 course project?
2. Your participation is voluntary. We will not collect unnecessary personal data. Is it okay if we take notes?

## Recent Experience
3. Tell us about the last time you needed help with a difficult subject or assignment.
4. What were you trying to achieve at that time?
5. What happened from start to finish when you looked for help or a tutor?

## Current Workaround
6. How do you usually find academic help or tutoring support?
7. What tools, apps, people, or methods do you currently use? (e.g., friends, seniors, Facebook groups, Line groups, private tutors)
8. What is difficult, slow, confusing, risky, or frustrating about the current method?

## Pain Severity and Consequence
9. How often do you need extra academic support or tutoring?
10. How serious is this problem on a scale of 1–5?
11. What happens if you cannot find suitable academic help or a tutor? (Ask friends, Study alone, Search online resources, Give up and Other: )

## Closing
12. What information would you like to see before choosing a tutor? (e.g., ratings, subjects, experience, availability, price)
13. May we use your anonymized response as part of our course project evidence?

# For Tutors (Students Providing Tutoring Services)

## Project Title
PeerTutor – Student-to-Student Tutoring Marketplace

## Target Respondent
University students who are interested in becoming tutors or have previously helped other students academically.

## Consent and Context
1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT105 course project?
2. Your participation is voluntary. We will not collect unnecessary personal data. Is it okay if we take notes?

## Recent Experience
3. Tell us about the last time you helped another student with a subject or assignment.
4. What subject did you help them with?
5. How did the student find and contact you?

## Current Workaround
6. How do students usually find you for tutoring or academic help? (e.g., Friends/Classmates, Seniors, Facebook groups, Line groups, etc.)
7. What tools, apps, people, or methods do you currently use to offer tutoring?
8. What is difficult, slow, confusing, risky, or frustrating about the current method? (e.g., Difficult to reach more students, etc.)

## Pain Severity and Consequence
9. How often do students ask for your help?
10. How serious is this problem on a scale of 1–5?
11. What happens if this problem is not solved? (Fewer students find me, Difficult to manage tutoring requests, etc.)

## Closing
12. What information would you like to show on your tutor profile? (e.g., Subjects taught, Experience, Ratings and reviews, etc.)
13. May we use your anonymized response as part of our course project evidence?
