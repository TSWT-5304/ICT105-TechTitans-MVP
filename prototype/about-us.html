Our Story

From a dorm room idea to a nationwide platform

PeerTutor started in 2022 when two MIT students — frustrated by expensive tutoring services and unhelpful office hours — decided to build something better. They created a simple platform where students who had aced a class could offer affordable sessions to those currently taking it.

Within three months, the platform had grown to 200 tutors across five universities. By 2024, we had expanded to 50+ campuses and facilitated over 50,000 sessions. Today, PeerTutor is the leading peer tutoring marketplace for university students in the United States.

We believe education should be accessible, personal, and peer-driven. Not corporate. Not overpriced. Just students helping students.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Vision

We're not just building a tutoring platform. We're reimagining how students learn from each other.

Our Mission

To make high-quality, affordable academic support accessible to every university student — by connecting them with peers who have already succeeded in their exact courses. We believe the best tutors aren't always professors; they're the students sitting next to you who just aced the exam.

Our Vision

A world where no student fails a class because they couldn't afford help or couldn't find the right support. We envision PeerTutor as the global standard for university peer learning — a platform where academic success is shared, not gatekept.

Our Values

What we stand for

Peer-First Learning

We believe students learn best from students. Relatability and shared experience create better outcomes than traditional tutoring.

Affordability

Quality academic help shouldn't cost a fortune. Our platform keeps rates fair for students on both sides of the session.

Trust & Safety

Every tutor is verified through their university, reviewed by students, and monitored for quality. You're always in safe hands.

Flexibility

Book sessions around your schedule. Last-minute session before an exam? No problem. We're built for real student life.

Community

PeerTutor is more than a marketplace. It's a community of students lifting each other up and paying it forward.

Continuous Growth

We're constantly improving the platform based on student and tutor feedback. Your experience shapes what we build next.
