FAQ

How do I cancel a session?

Go to My Dashboard → Upcoming Sessions → Cancel. Cancellations made 24+ hours before are free.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are automatic if you cancel 24+ hrs before the session, or if the tutor cancels.

Can I change my tutor after booking?

Cancel your current session and rebook with a different tutor. Cancellation policy applies.

How do I report a problem with a tutor?

Contact our support team via the Contact Us page and we'll investigate within 24 hours.
